GERMANY: King Abdullah II Meets With Chancellor Angela Merkel

King Abdullah II of Jordan met with German Chancellor Angela Merkel on June 24 in Berlin. During a press conference before the meeting the king mentioned the “burden of the refugee traffic” in his country and said that Jordan is looking to the international community for assistance to “continue to be an island of stability in a very volatile region”. He also addressed challenges to stability in Iraq and Syria and expressed hope for an opportunity to relaunch Palestinian-Israeli negotiations for a peace process.
